About the Artist

Dad has been painting for over thirty years, finding inspiration in the landscapes, seascapes, and quiet corners of everyday life. Working primarily in oils and acrylics, each piece captures a moment of light and color that tells its own story.

What started as a weekend hobby has grown into a lifelong passion. From sunrise sketches at the harbor to golden-hour landscapes in the countryside, every painting reflects a deep appreciation for the natural world and the simple beauty found in ordinary places.

This gallery is a collection of that work — shared here for family, friends, and anyone who finds joy in art.
